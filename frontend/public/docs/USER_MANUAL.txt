## 🔑 1. Initial Setup (For Beginners)

When you launch the app for the **first time**, you will see a **Setup** screen asking for **API Keys** and a **PIN**. This only happens once.

### 💡 What are API Keys?
They are "keys" that allow the app to connect with Artificial Intelligence (AI) models to power Translation, Speech Recognition, and Pronunciation features.

| Key Name | Main Role | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OpenRouter API Key** | Translates, explains grammar, and chats with you. | **Required** 🔴 |
| **Deepgram API Key** | Converts video audio to text for learning. | **Required** 🔴 |
| **Minimax API Key** | Pronounces words/sentences (AI Voice). | **Required** 🔴 |
| **Azure Speech Key & Region** | Grades your pronunciation word by word. | **Required** 🔴 |

> ⚠️ **Absolute Security**: All keys are encrypted and **only saved on your computer**. The app never sends them to our servers.

### 🔑 How to Get API Keys

#### 1. OpenRouter API Key (Translation & Explanation)
*   See tutorial here: https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/Create-API-Key-in-OpenRouter-95b3cf6d85df49b48ad728d93c8d5b76

#### 2. Deepgram API Key (Speech-to-Text)
*   See tutorial here: https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/Create-API-Key-in-Deepgram-d2eab0f8d3c84b8ab315964c935c3945

#### 3. Azure Speech Key & Region (Pronunciation Grading)
*   **Step 1**: Go to [portal.azure.com](https://portal.azure.com/).
*   **Step 2**: Search for **"Speech Services"** and select it.
*   **Step 3**: Click **Create** → Fill in Subscription, Resource Group, Region, Name.
    *   *Tip*: Choose the **Free F0** tier if available.
*   **Step 4**: After creating, open the resource → Click **Keys and Endpoint** in the left menu.
*   **Step 5**: You will find **Key 1** (your API Key) and **Location** (your Region). Note both.

---

### 🔐 Create a PIN
- Create a PIN (minimum 4 characters) and confirm it.
- The PIN encrypts your keys and lesson data while the app is closed.

> 🚨 **Important**: If you forget your PIN, you will need to reset the app and re-enter all API keys. There is no recovery option. Write your PIN somewhere safe!

## 🔓 2. Daily Use: Unlocking the App

Every time you re-open ShadowLearn, you will see the **Unlock** screen. Simply enter your PIN to access your lessons and data.

> This is normal — it means your data is protected when you're not using the app.

## 📚 3. Library & Lesson Management

The main dashboard is the **Library**, containing all your created lessons.

![Library](docs/images/library.png)

### ➕ Creating a New Lesson
Click the **+** card or **Create Lesson** button. You have 2 options:

1. **Via YouTube Link** 🌐:
   - Paste the video URL into the box.
   - The app will download the audio and generate subtitles automatically.
2. **Upload from Computer** 📁:
   - Upload an audio (.mp3) or video (.mp4) file from your device.

### 🌐 Choose Translation Language
- Select your target language (e.g., **English**).
- Click **Generate**. The lesson enters a processing queue — you can return to the Library to wait (status shown as *Processing*).

![Create New Lesson](docs/images/create_lesson.png)

### 🗂️ Managing Existing Lessons

Each lesson card in the Library supports:

- **Rename** ✏️ — Click the pencil icon on a card to give it a new name.
- **Delete** 🗑️ — Remove the lesson and all its data permanently.
- **Retry** 🔄 — If a YouTube lesson failed to process, use Retry to try again.
- **Search** 🔍 — Use the search bar at the top to filter lessons by title.

## 🖥️ 4. Learning Interface (Classroom)

Opening a lesson divides the screen into **3 main columns**:

![Shadowing Screen](docs/images/shadowing_screen.png)

### 📺 Left Column — Video / Audio Player

Plays the lesson content. Controls include:

- **Play / Pause** button
- **Playback speed**: Choose 0.5×, 0.75×, 1×, 1.25×, or 1.5× — slower speeds are great for beginners
- **Seek bar**: Click anywhere on the timeline to jump to that moment
- **Volume slider**: Adjust audio volume
- **Download** ⬇️: Save the video/audio file to your computer
- **Rename** ✏️: Click the pencil icon next to the lesson title to rename it

### 📜 Middle Column — Subtitles & Practice

- Displays all segments with **Pinyin**, **Chinese text**, and **translation**.
- **Click any segment** to jump the video to that exact moment.
- **Hover a word** to see its pronunciation and a bookmark icon.
- **Bookmark 🔖**: Save any word to your Vocabulary Workbook.
- **Play 🔊**: Hear the AI pronounce any segment.
- **Copy 📋**: Copy the segment text to clipboard.
- **Shadow 🎯**: Start a Shadowing session beginning from that specific segment.
- **Shadowing button** 🎤 at the top: Start a full Shadowing session for the lesson.

### 🤖 Right Column — AI Companion (2 Tabs)

**Tab 1 — Chat 💬**
- Ask the AI anything about the current lesson: grammar, vocabulary, context, cultural notes.
- Example questions: *"What grammar pattern is used here?"*, *"What does X mean in this context?"*
- The AI is aware of which segment you are currently on.

**Tab 2 — Workbook 📚**
- Shows a mini-view of words you have saved from this lesson.
- Click any word to jump back to where it was used in the transcript.
- **Study This Lesson** button at the bottom opens a quiz session for this lesson's saved words.

## 🎤 5. Shadowing Practice (Pronunciation & Listening)

Click the **Shadowing** button in the middle column (or the 🎯 icon on a specific segment). A dialog will appear where you choose your mode and how many segments to practice.

### Choosing a Mode

![Shadowing Modes](docs/images/shadowing_modes.png)

| Mode | What You Do |
| :--- | :--- |
| 🔤 **Dictation Mode** | Listen to a segment and type what you hear |
| 🎤 **Speaking Mode** | Listen to a segment and speak it aloud into your microphone |

You can also set **how many segments** to practice, or choose "All remaining" to go through the whole lesson.

---

### 🔤 Dictation Mode Flow

**Step 1 — Listen Phase**
- The segment audio plays automatically.
- Focus on the sounds, rhythm, and tone. Use the **Replay** button or press **Space** to hear it again.

**Step 2 — Type Phase**
- A text input appears. Type exactly what you heard.
- Click **Submit** when done.

**Step 3 — Reveal Phase**
- Your answer is shown alongside the correct text.
- Each character is highlighted: 🟢 Green = correct, 🔴 Red = wrong.
- Your accuracy score is shown.
- Click **Retry** to try again or **Next** to move on.

![Dictation Result](docs/images/shadowing_result.png)

---

### 🎤 Speaking Mode Flow

**Step 1 — Listen Phase**
- The segment audio plays automatically. Press **Space** or **Replay** to hear it again.

**Step 2 — Speak Phase**
- Click the **microphone button** (or press **Space**) to start recording.
- Speak the segment aloud clearly, then click again to stop.
- You can play back your recording before submitting, or click **Redo** to re-record.
- Click **Submit** when satisfied.

**Step 3 — Reveal Phase**
- Your recording is played back alongside the correct AI pronunciation.
- Azure grades your pronunciation: 🟢 Green = good, 🔴 Red = needs work.
- Click **Retry** to try again or **Next** to continue.

---

### 📊 Session Summary

After all segments are done, you see a full summary:
- Accuracy score per segment
- Which segments you attempted, skipped, or were auto-skipped (very short segments are skipped automatically)
- Overall session average score

## 📓 6. Vocabulary Workbook & Quizzes

### 📌 Saving Words
While reading subtitles, **hover over any word** and click the **Bookmark 🔖** icon. The word is instantly added to your Workbook.

### 📖 Accessing the Full Workbook

Click **Workbook** in the top navigation to open the full Vocabulary page. Here you can:

- See **total words saved** and how many lessons they come from
- **Search** for any word by character, pinyin, or meaning
- Browse words **grouped by lesson** — click a group to expand or collapse it
- Click **Play 🔊** on any word to hear it pronounced
- Click **Study** on any lesson group to practice only those words

![Workbook Dashboard](docs/images/workbook_dashboard.png)

---

### 🎮 Quiz Formats

When you start a study session (from the Workbook or the lesson's Workbook tab), choose your exercise type and how many words to practice:

| Quiz Mode | What You Do | Skill Trained |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🔀 **Mixed** | Randomly cycles through all exercise types | All-round practice |
| 📝 **Cloze** | Fill in blanks in an AI-generated story | Reading in context |
| 🔤 **Dictation** | Listen and type the Chinese sentence | Listening & spelling |
| 🎤 **Pronunciation** | Read the sentence aloud for Azure to grade | Speaking accuracy |
| 🧩 **Reconstruction** | Arrange jumbled words into the correct sentence | Grammar & word order |
| 🧠 **Pinyin Recall** | See the character — can you recall its pinyin? | Reading & pronunciation |
| ✍️ **Character Writing** | Trace the stroke order of each character on screen | Writing & muscle memory |

> 💡 Each exercise has an **ⓘ info button** — click it to read a description of the exercise before starting.

![Study Session](docs/images/study_workbook.png)

After finishing, a **Session Summary** shows your score breakdown by exercise type.

## ⚙️ 7. Settings

Click the **Settings icon** (⚙️) in the top navigation at any time to:

- **Update API Keys** — paste new or corrected keys; use the 👁️ icon to show/hide a key
- **Change PIN** — enter a new PIN and confirm it
- **Set Default Translation Language** — choose which language lessons translate into by default
- **Lock App** — immediately lock the app (requires PIN to re-open)

> 🚨 **Forgot PIN button**: This resets the app and erases all stored API keys. You will need to re-enter them on next launch. Use only as a last resort.

## ❓ 8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Why don't I see pronunciation scores?**
- **A:** Check that your **Azure Speech Key** and **Region** are correctly entered in Settings. Open Settings from the ⚙️ icon anytime to update them.

**Q: Can I change my PIN?**
- **A:** Yes — open Settings and use the **Change PIN** section. Write your PIN down somewhere safe; there is no recovery if forgotten.

**Q: How long does YouTube lesson creation take?**
- **A:** Usually 1–3 minutes depending on video length (download + transcription + translation).

**Q: The lesson shows an error. What do I do?**
- **A:** For YouTube lessons, click **Retry** on the lesson card in the Library. For uploaded files, delete the lesson and upload again.

**Q: Why are some segments skipped in Shadowing mode?**
- **A:** Very short segments (fewer than 3 words) are automatically skipped — they are too brief to practice meaningfully.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
- **A:** Everything — lessons, vocabulary, chat history, audio cache — is stored locally in your browser's storage. Nothing is sent to our servers.
