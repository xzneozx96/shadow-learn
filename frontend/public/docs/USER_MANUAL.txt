# Welcome to ShadowLearn!

## Overview

**ShadowLearn** is your ultimate companion to conquer any foreign language using the **Shadowing** technique (Listen & Repeat) combined with smart interactive exercises and AI Assistance.

This guide will walk you through setting up and maximizing the application's features.

---

## 1. Initial Setup (For Beginners)

When you launch the app for the **first time**, you will see a **Setup** screen asking for **API Keys** and a **PIN**. This only happens once.

### What are API Keys?
They are "keys" that allow the app to connect with Artificial Intelligence (AI) models to power Translation, Speech Recognition, and Pronunciation features.

| Key Name | Main Role | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OpenRouter API Key** | Translates, explains grammar, and chats with you. | **Optional (recommended)**  |
| **Google Gemini API Key** | Powers live voice conversations in Speak with AI. | **Required for Speak with AI**  |

> ️ **Absolute Security**: All keys are encrypted and **only saved on your computer**. The app never sends them to our servers.

>  **No OpenRouter key? You can still start right away.** Leave the field blank and ShadowLearn falls back to a **shared key pooled with other learners**, so you can try every feature for free. Heads-up: this shared key is a goodwill free trial and **may slow down, get paused, or be discontinued** as the community grows beyond what the author can afford to fund. For reliable, uninterrupted learning, we strongly recommend adding **your own** OpenRouter key in Setup or Settings — it stays encrypted on your device just like every other key.

### How to Get API Keys

#### OpenRouter API Key (Translation & Explanation)
*   See tutorial here: https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/Create-API-Key-in-OpenRouter-95b3cf6d85df49b48ad728d93c8d5b76

#### Google Gemini API Key (Speak with AI)

> **Video walkthrough:** https://app.tango.us/app/workflow/Create-Google-AI-Studio-API-Key-ac110f276682495784c9fa2998323bee

1. Go to [aistudio.google.com](https://aistudio.google.com) and sign in with your Google account
2. Click **Get API key** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Create API key** → select **Create API key in new project** → copy the key (starts with `AIza…`)
4. Still inside AI Studio, open **Billing** from the left menu → click **Add payment method** → enter your **Visa / Mastercard** details and confirm. ️ **Do not use the free trial** — it is unstable and will cause the voice session to drop or fail. Paid billing is required for reliable Gemini Live performance.
5. Open ShadowLearn → **Settings** → paste the key into the **Google Gemini (Realtime)** field and save

![Google AI Studio — API key and billing setup](docs/images/speak_google_api_key.png)

>  **Cost:** ~300 VNĐ (~$0.012 USD) per minute of conversation. You only pay when you use it.

### Create a PIN
- Create a PIN (minimum 4 characters) and confirm it.
- The PIN encrypts your keys and lesson data while the app is closed.

>  **Important**: If you forget your PIN, you will need to reset the app and re-enter all API keys. There is no recovery option. Write your PIN somewhere safe!

## 3. Library & Lesson Management

The main dashboard is the **Library**, containing all your created lessons.

![Library](docs/images/library.png)

### Creating a New Lesson
Click the **+** card or **Create Lesson** button. You have 3 options:

1. **Via YouTube Link** :
   - Paste the video URL into the box.
   - The app will download the audio and generate subtitles automatically.
2. **Upload from Computer** :
   - Upload an audio (.mp3) or video (.mp4) file from your device.
3. **From a Blog or News Article** :
   - Paste the link to any article you find interesting. ShadowLearn extracts the text and turns it into a natural-sounding **audio podcast** — complete with narration, an aligned transcript, and vocabulary breakdowns for shadowing practice.
   - **No link, or behind a login wall?** Switch to **Paste Text** mode and drop in up to 2,000 characters directly. We synthesize the voice, build the transcript, and translate it for you.

Select your target language (e.g., **English**), then click **Generate**. The lesson enters a processing queue — you can return to the Library to wait (status shown as *Processing*).

> 🎬 **Study with a vibe:** Blog and article lessons play over a looping ambient background video. Pick a scene from the strip at the bottom, and click anywhere on the video to play or pause.

- **Delete** ️ — Remove the lesson and all its data permanently.

## 5. AI Companion — Your Personal Language Tutor

> *Most language apps give you exercises. ShadowLearn gives you a tutor.*

The **AI Companion** lives in the right panel of every lesson. It is not a simple chatbot — it is an intelligent, agentic tutor that **knows who you are**, **remembers you across sessions**, and **takes action** on your behalf. It can read your vocabulary list, check which words you're due to review, see exactly which sentence you're studying, and then launch a real interactive exercise — all directly inside the chat.

### What Makes It Different

| Ordinary chatbot | ShadowLearn AI Companion |
| :--- | :--- |
| Answers questions | Answers questions **and acts on them** |
| Forgets you next session | **Remembers** your preferences, struggles, and goals |
| Gives generic advice | Adapts to **your level and progress** in real time |
| Talks about exercises | **Runs exercises directly in the chat** |

### How to Start a Conversation

Just type naturally. Here are ideas to get you going:

**Ask about the current sentence:**
- *"What does this sentence mean?"*
- *"Break down the grammar of this line for me."*
- *"What's the difference between 了 (le) and 过 (guò)?"*

**Ask for practice:**
- *"Quiz me on the words I saved from this lesson."*
- *"Give me a dictation exercise."*
- *"Can we practice the pronunciation of this sentence?"*
- *"I keep forgetting 尤其 — can we drill it?"*

**Ask for a progress check:**
- *"How am I doing overall?"*
- *"Show me my accuracy trend."*
- *"What words should I review today?"*

**Tell it about yourself:**
- *"I'm a complete beginner, please keep explanations simple."*
- *"My goal is to pass HSK 3 by the end of the year."*
- *"I struggle with tones — focus on that when you quiz me."*

The AI will remember everything you tell it, even in future sessions.

### Interactive Exercises Right in the Chat

This is the standout feature. When you ask for practice, the AI doesn't just describe an exercise — it **renders a live, interactive exercise directly inside the conversation**. You complete it, submit your answer, and the AI responds with feedback and updates your progress automatically.

Exercises the AI can launch in chat:

| Exercise | What happens |
| :--- | :--- |
|  **Dictation** | An audio clip plays; you type what you hear |
| ️ **Character Writing** | A character appears; you trace its stroke order |
|  **Pinyin Recall** | A character appears; you type its pinyin |
|  **Translation** | A sentence appears; you translate it |
|  **Pronunciation** | A sentence appears; you record yourself saying it |
|  **Cloze (Fill-in-the-blank)** | The AI writes a short story using your words with gaps to fill |
|  **Reconstruction** | Word chips appear scrambled; you drag them into the right order |

The AI chooses which words to include based on what you've saved, what's due for review, and what you've been struggling with recently — not randomly.

### It Knows You — The Learner Profile

The first time you start chatting, the AI will ask you a few quick questions:
- What is your current level?
- What is your native language?
- What is your daily study goal?

These answers build your **Learner Profile**, which the AI reads at the start of every session to calibrate the difficulty of its explanations and exercise selections. You can update it at any time simply by telling the AI — *"I think I'm at intermediate level now"* — and it will adjust.

### Long-Term Memory ️

The AI keeps a **persistent memory** of important things you share with it:
- Your learning goals and motivations
- Topics or grammar patterns you find difficult
- Preferences for how you like to be taught
- Observations it makes about your performance over time

This memory carries over to every future session. The AI can also search its own memory when you ask — *"What have I told you about my goals?"* — and retrieve what it knows about you.

### Ask for Your Progress

At any time, type:
- *"Show me my progress chart."*
- *"How is my skill breakdown looking?"*

The AI will render a **visual chart** right in the chat — an accuracy trend graph or a mastery breakdown across all five skills (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing, Vocabulary).

### Tips for Getting the Best Out of It

- **Be specific.** *"Quiz me on 想 and 觉得"* works better than *"quiz me"*.
- **Tell it your mood.** *"I only have 5 minutes"* → it will keep the session short.
- **Ask it to explain differently.** If an explanation doesn't click, say *"explain that another way"*.
- **Trust its suggestions.** When it says *"you have 3 words due for review"*, take the quiz — spaced repetition works best when you don't skip.

## 6. Speak with AI — Live Voice Practice ️

> *No scheduling. No judgment. Just you and an AI that corrects your grammar while you speak.*

**Speak with AI** is a real-time voice conversation feature. You speak — the AI responds in your target language — and the app gives you instant support so you never get stuck. Before using this feature, make sure you've added your **Google Gemini API key** in Settings (see [Initial Setup ↑](#1-initial-setup-for-beginners)).

### What You Get in Every Session

| Feature | What it does |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Voice conversation** | Speak freely; AI responds naturally in your target language |
| **Grammar feedback** | Every message silently analyzed — tap it to see corrections |
| **Next-Line Suggestion** | Can't think of what to say? AI suggests your next line |
| **Cultural tips** | Notes on idioms, formality, and cultural nuance |
| **Vocabulary checklist** | Target words auto-ticked as you use them |
| **Session recap** | Full summary with all grammar corrections and an overall evaluation |

**Supported languages:** Chinese (Simplified), English, Japanese.
**Session limit:** 10 minutes per session (countdown timer shown during conversation).

### How to Use It

#### Step 1 — Open Speak with AI
There are two ways to access the feature:

- **From the Topbar:** Click the **Speak with AI** button in the left navigation menu.
- **From the Companion Panel:** Inside a lesson, click the **Voice** icon in the AI Companion panel on the right.

If your Google Gemini key isn't set yet, the app will prompt you to go to Settings first.

#### Step 2 — Choose language and level
Select your **target language**, then pick a **proficiency level**:

-  **Beginner** — HSK 1-2 / CEFR A1-A2 / JLPT N5-N4 / TOPIK I
-  **Intermediate** — HSK 3-4 / CEFR B1-B2 / JLPT N3-N2 / TOPIK II 3-4
-  **Advanced** — HSK 5-6 / CEFR C1-C2 / JLPT N1 / TOPIK II 5-6

The AI adjusts vocabulary, sentence complexity, and feedback depth to match your level. Click **Continue**.

![Language and level picker](docs/images/speak_language_level.png)

#### Step 3 — Pick a persona
Choose the AI character you want to talk to. Each persona has a distinct personality and speaking style — friendly study buddy, encouraging friend, strict parent, taxi driver, and more. Available personas vary by language.

![Persona picker — grid of characters](docs/images/speak_persona_picker.png)

#### Step 4 — Pick a situation
Choose a scenario from the grid: casual chat, ordering food, asking directions, shopping/bargaining, job interview, meeting the family, hospital visit, karaoke, and more.

**Can't find the right scenario?** Click ** Create your own** at the top of the grid. Describe your situation in plain text and the AI generates a custom scenario with matching vocabulary.

![Situation picker](docs/images/speak_situation_picker.png)

#### Step 5 — Preview and confirm
Before starting, review the scenario card: description, your role and goal, and the target vocabulary for this session. Click **Regenerate** for a fresh version, or **Start** when ready.

![Situation preview card](docs/images/speak_situation_preview.png)

#### Step 6 — Have the conversation

The session screen has three panels:

**Center — Conversation**
- AI avatar with a mic ring that pulses when the AI is speaking
- Live transcript of the full conversation
- **Hold microphone button to speak, release when done**
- Session countdown timer from 10:00

**Right — Intelligence Panel**
-  **Target vocabulary checklist** — words get crossed off as you use them
-  **Next-Line Suggestion** — a contextual prompt to help continue the conversation
-  **Cultural tips** — idioms, register, situational etiquette
-  **Vocab tips** — quick explanations of words the AI just used

**Left — Grammar Feedback**
- Click any message in the transcript to open its grammar panel
- Corrections shown as: ~~original~~ → **corrected**, with explanation

![Active session — all three panels labeled](docs/images/speak_active_session.png)

>  **Tips:** Speak at a natural pace. If you go silent, the AI gently prompts you. Use Next-Line Suggestion when stuck — say it aloud and see how the AI responds. Grammar corrections appear automatically.

When finished, click **End Session**.

#### Step 7 — Review your recap
After the session, a full recap shows:
- Duration and turn count
- Overall AI evaluation of your performance
- Strengths and areas to improve
- Grammar correction timeline — every correction from the session, in order

Click **Practice Again** to repeat the same scenario, or **Back** to choose a new one.

![Session recap](docs/images/speak_session_recap.png)

### Troubleshooting

**"Google API key required" error** → Go to Settings and add your Google Gemini API key.

**Microphone not working** → In Chrome/Edge: click the lock icon in the address bar → allow microphone.

**Session won't start / loading spinner hangs** → Check your internet connection. The session requires a stable connection to the voice server.

**Choppy or delayed audio** → Close other tabs using audio. Move closer to your router or switch to a wired connection.

## 7. Shadowing Practice (Pronunciation & Listening)

Click the **Shadowing** button in the middle column (or the  icon on a specific segment). A dialog will appear where you choose your mode and how many segments to practice.

### Choosing a Mode

![Shadowing Modes](docs/images/shadowing_modes.png)

| Mode | What You Do |
| :--- | :--- |
|  **Dictation Mode** | Listen to a segment and type what you hear |
|  **Speaking Mode** | Listen to a segment and speak it aloud into your microphone |

You can also set **how many segments** to practice, or choose "All remaining" to go through the whole lesson.

### Dictation Mode Flow

**Step 1 — Listen Phase**
- The segment audio plays automatically.
- Focus on the sounds, rhythm, and tone. Use the **Replay** button or press **Space** to hear it again.

**Step 2 — Type Phase**
- A text input appears. Type exactly what you heard.
- Click **Submit** when done.

**Step 3 — Reveal Phase**
- Your answer is shown alongside the correct text.
- Each character is highlighted:  Green = correct,  Red = wrong.
- Your accuracy score is shown.
- Click **Retry** to try again or **Next** to move on.

![Dictation Result](docs/images/shadowing_result.png)

### Speaking Mode Flow

**Step 1 — Listen Phase**
- The segment audio plays automatically. Press **Space** or **Replay** to hear it again.

**Step 2 — Speak Phase**
- Click the **microphone button** (or press **Space**) to start recording.
- Speak the segment aloud clearly, then click again to stop.
- You can play back your recording before submitting, or click **Redo** to re-record.
- Click **Submit** when satisfied.

**Step 3 — Reveal Phase**
- Your recording is played back alongside the correct AI pronunciation.
- Azure grades your pronunciation:  Green = good,  Red = needs work.
- Click **Retry** to try again or **Next** to continue.

### Shadowing Session Summary

After all segments are done, you see a full summary:
- Accuracy score per segment
- Which segments you attempted, skipped, or were auto-skipped (very short segments are skipped automatically)
- Overall session average score

## 8. Vocabulary Workbook, Quizzes & Progress

### Saving Words
While reading subtitles, **hover over any word** and click the **Bookmark ** icon. The word is instantly added to your Workbook.

### Word Breakdown — Decode Characters, Anchor Memories 📖

Ever stared at a Chinese character and drawn a complete blank? **Word Breakdown** gives you X-ray vision into any character. Click the **book icon (📖)** on any vocabulary card to open it:

- **Radicals** — the semantic roots that hint at a word's meaning.
- **Components** — every sub-part of the character, named and explained.
- **Hán Việt (Sino-Vietnamese)** — the bridge linking characters to Vietnamese vocabulary you may already know.
- **AI memory story** — the moment you open the panel, the AI weaves the components into a vivid, memorable scene so the word sticks. Hit **🔄** for a fresh story, or **✏️** to write your own. Your story saves to the card, ready for every future review.

![Word Breakdown — radical breakdown and AI memory story](docs/images/word_breakdown_demo.png)

### Accessing the Full Workbook

Click **Workbook** in the top navigation to open the full Vocabulary page. Here you can:

- See **total words saved** and how many lessons they come from
- **Search** for any word by character, pinyin, or meaning
- Browse words **grouped by lesson** — click a group to expand or collapse it
- Click **Play ** on any word to hear it pronounced
- Click **Study** on any lesson group to practice only those words

![Workbook Dashboard](docs/images/workbook_dashboard.png)

### Quiz Formats

When you start a study session (from the Workbook or the lesson's Workbook tab), choose your exercise type and how many words to practice:

| Quiz Mode | What You Do | Skill Trained |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
|  **Mixed** | Randomly cycles through all exercise types | All-round practice |
|  **Cloze** | Fill in blanks in an AI-generated story | Reading in context |
|  **Dictation** | Listen and type the Chinese sentence | Listening & spelling |
|  **Pronunciation** | Read the sentence aloud for Azure to grade | Speaking accuracy |
|  **Reconstruction** | Arrange jumbled words into the correct sentence | Grammar & word order |
|  **Pinyin Recall** | See the character — can you recall its pinyin? | Reading & pronunciation |
| ️ **Character Writing** | Trace the stroke order of each character on screen | Writing & muscle memory |

>  Each exercise has an **ⓘ info button** — click it to read a description of the exercise before starting.

![Study Session](docs/images/study_workbook.png)

After finishing, a **Session Summary** shows your score breakdown by exercise type.

### Progress Dashboard

The bottom of the Workbook page shows a live progress dashboard that updates automatically as you complete exercises.

#### Overall Stats
Displays your total exercise count, overall accuracy rate, and total study time accumulated across all sessions.

#### Accuracy Trend
A chart showing your daily accuracy over the past 90 days — one data point per day. It reflects the running accuracy of all exercises you answered that day.

#### Skill Breakdown
Five cards — **Writing**, **Speaking**, **Vocabulary**, **Reading**, **Listening** — each showing:
- Accuracy percentage (correct answers ÷ total attempts for that skill)
- Total exercise attempts logged

![Progress Tracking](docs/images/progress_tracking.png)

Each exercise type feeds into a specific skill:

| Exercise | Skill tracked |
| :--- | :--- |
| ️ Character Writing | Writing |
|  Translation | Writing |
|  Pronunciation | Speaking |
|  Pinyin Recall | Speaking |
|  Cloze | Vocabulary |
|  Reconstruction | Reading |
|  Dictation | Listening |

>  **How scoring works:** Binary exercises (Dictation, Cloze, Reconstruction, Pinyin Recall) score 100 for correct or 0 for wrong. Character Writing scores 100 if no hints were used, 80 if hints were used. Translation and Pronunciation receive real numeric scores (0–100) from AI. A score of **60 or above** counts as a correct answer for accuracy purposes.

#### Common Mistakes
Highlights vocabulary items you have struggled with most frequently, so you know where to focus.

#### Spaced Repetition & Review Queue
Every time you answer an exercise, ShadowLearn schedules the next review for that vocabulary word using a scientifically-proven method that spaces reviews over increasing intervals based on how well you know the word:

- Answer correctly and confidently → next review in several days
- Answer incorrectly → reviewed again soon (tomorrow or the day after)
- Consistently correct over 5 sessions → the word levels up and is reviewed less often

When words are due for review, a banner appears at the top of the Workbook page: **"X items due for review today"**. Click **Start Review** to launch a targeted session with only those words.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### API Keys & Cost

**Q: Do I really need an OpenRouter key to start?**
- **A:** No. Leave it blank and ShadowLearn falls back to a **shared key pooled with other learners**, so you can try everything for free. It's a goodwill trial, though — it may slow down or be paused as the community grows. For reliable, uninterrupted use, add your own key in Settings.

**Q: What does my own OpenRouter key cost?**
- **A:** You pay OpenRouter directly, only for what you use. Translation, grammar explanations, quizzes, and chat are inexpensive — typically a few cents of usage goes a long way. You stay in full control of your spending limits on OpenRouter.

**Q: Which features need the Google Gemini key?**
- **A:** Only **Speak with AI** (live voice). Everything else runs on OpenRouter (or the shared key). Gemini Live costs ~300 VNĐ (~$0.012) per minute and requires paid billing — the free trial tier is unstable and drops sessions.

**Q: Are my API keys safe?**
- **A:** Yes. Every key is encrypted with your PIN and **stored only on your device**. They are never sent to our servers.

### Lessons & Library

**Q: What kinds of content can I turn into a lesson?**
- **A:** Three sources: a **YouTube link**, an **audio/video file** from your computer, or a **blog/news article** (paste the URL — or paste up to 2,000 characters of text directly). Articles become a narrated audio podcast with transcript and vocabulary.

**Q: How long does lesson creation take?**
- **A:** Usually 1–3 minutes, depending on length (download/extraction + transcription + translation). You can leave the Library and come back — the status shows *Processing*.

**Q: A lesson shows an error. What do I do?**
- **A:** For YouTube/article lessons, click **Retry** on the lesson card. For uploaded files, delete the lesson and upload again.

**Q: What's the difference between a lesson and Explore?**
- **A:** A **lesson** is for shadowing and exercises (transcript, pinyin, practice). **Explore** is a study hub that turns a YouTube video into an AI study guide — summary, mind map, study guide, flashcards, chat, and notes — focused on grammar and vocabulary rather than shadowing.

### Vocabulary & Word Breakdown

**Q: How do I save a word, and where does it go?**
- **A:** Hover any word in the transcript and click the **bookmark** icon. It lands in your **Workbook**, grouped by lesson, ready for quizzes and review.

**Q: What is the book icon (📖) on a vocabulary card?**
- **A:** It opens **Word Breakdown** — radicals, components, the Hán Việt reading, and an AI-generated memory story. Tap 🔄 for a new story or ✏️ to write your own; it saves to the card.

### Smart Study & Review

**Q: What is the floating badge that follows me around?**
- **A:** That's **Smart Study**. Tap it to open your daily dashboard — reviews, shadowing, and custom tasks in one place.

**Q: How does ShadowLearn decide what I review each day?**
- **A:** **Spaced Repetition (SRS).** Each word is scheduled to resurface right before you'd forget it — answer well and it returns later; miss it and it comes back soon. When words are due, a banner offers **Start Review**.

### AI Companion & Memory

**Q: The AI Companion doesn't seem to know my lesson / vocabulary.**
- **A:** It looks up your data on demand. Just ask directly — *"quiz me on my saved words"* — and it fetches them.

**Q: Does the AI Companion remember me between sessions?**
- **A:** Yes — it has long-term memory. Tell it your goals, level, or weak spots and it remembers for future sessions.

### Speak with AI & Shadowing

**Q: Speak with AI won't start.**
- **A:** Check that your Google Gemini key is set in Settings, and that your internet connection is stable — the voice session needs a live connection to the server.

**Q: Why are some segments skipped in Shadowing?**
- **A:** Very short segments (fewer than 3 words) are skipped automatically — too brief to practice meaningfully.

### Account & Security

- **A:** Yes — Settings → **Change PIN**. Write it down somewhere safe; there is no recovery if forgotten.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
- **A:** Everything — lessons, vocabulary, chat history, AI memory, audio cache — lives locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers.
