# Skill Guide: Grammar — Directional Complements (方向补语)

A directional complement follows a verb to express **the direction of movement** of an action. Vietnamese has no equivalent structure. Requires explicit teaching — do not expect learners to infer it from context.

There are two tiers: **simple** (单纯趋向补语) and **compound** (复合趋向补语).

---

## Tier 1: Simple Directional Complements — 来 and 去

| Complement | Core meaning | Speaker position |
|---|---|---|
| 来 lái | Movement **toward** speaker | Speaker is the destination |
| 去 qù | Movement **away from** speaker | Speaker is the origin |

**Examples:**
- 你出去吧！(Nǐ chūqù ba) — Go outside! *(speaker is inside → action moves away)*
- 我回来了！(Wǒ huílái le) — I'm back! *(speaker/listener is at home → movement toward)*
- 你进去吧！(Nǐ jìnqù ba) — Go inside. *(speaker is outside → action moves away)*
- 你别进来，我马上出去。(Nǐ bié jìnlái, wǒ mǎshàng chūqù) — Don't come in, I'll come out right away.
- 小王回学校去了。(Xiǎowáng huí xuéxiào qù le) — Xiao Wang went back to school.
- 快进家里来吧。(Kuài jìn jiālǐ lái ba) — Come into the house quickly.

**Simple directional complements are motion verbs** (回 huí, 进 jìn, 出 chū, 上 shàng, 下 xià, 过 guò, 起 qǐ, 开 kāi, etc.) + 来/去.

---

## Tier 2: Compound Directional Complements

Nine combinations formed by pairing a motion verb with 来 or 去. Each carries both a **literal meaning** and one or more **extended meanings**.

| Compound | Literal direction |
|---|---|
| 上来 shànglái | Up toward speaker |
| 上去 shàngqù | Up away from speaker |
| 下来 xiàlái | Down toward speaker |
| 下去 xiàqù | Down away from speaker |
| 出来 chūlái | Out toward speaker |
| 出去 chūqù | Out away from speaker |
| 过来 guòlái | Across/over toward speaker |
| 过去 guòqù | Across/over away from speaker |
| 起来 qǐlái | Up from a lower position |

---

### 上来 shànglái — Up toward speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move upward toward speaker (speaker is higher) | 小明快跑上楼来。(Xiǎomíng kuài pǎo shàng lóu lái) — Xiao Ming, run up here quickly. |
| Status elevation — speaker is at higher level | 小王是从基层调上来的。(Xiǎowáng shì cóng jīcéng diào shànglái de) — Xiao Wang was promoted from the grassroots level. |
| Successfully complete an action; catch up | 经过努力，我的学习已经跟上来了。(Jīngguò nǔlì, wǒ de xuéxí yǐjīng gēn shànglái le) — Through hard work, my studies have caught up. |
| Ability to achieve something (with 得/不) | 每个问题都答得上来。(Měi gè wèntí dōu dá de shànglái) — Can answer every question. |

---

### 上去 shàngqù — Up away from speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move upward away from speaker (speaker is lower) | 不，我不上去，你下来吧。(Bù, wǒ bù shàngqù, nǐ xiàlái ba) — No, I won't go up; you come down. |
| Submit upward — speaker is at lower level | 我的作业交上去吧。(Wǒ de zuòyè jiāo shàngqù ba) — I'm submitting my homework. / 我们的问题已经反映上去了。(Wǒmen de wèntí yǐjīng fǎnyìng shàngqù le) — Our issue has been reported up the chain. |
| Determined effort to improve a result | 这学期，玛丽的成绩一定要搞上去。(Zhè xuéqī, Mǎlì de chéngjī yīdìng yào gǎo shàngqù) — Mary's grades must improve this semester. |
| Attach or add something to a surface | 玲玲把画儿挂上去了。(Línglíng bǎ huàr guà shàngqù le) — Lingling hung the picture up on the wall. |

---

### 下来 xiàlái — Down toward speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move downward toward speaker (speaker is lower) | 我们不上去了，你下来吧。(Wǒmen bù shàngqù le, nǐ xiàlái ba) — We're not going up; come down here. |
| Distribute downward — speaker is at lower level | 老师把咱们的作业发下来了。(Lǎoshī bǎ zánmen de zuòyè fā xiàlái le) — The teacher handed down the homework. / 工作已经分配下来了。(Gōngzuò yǐjīng fēnpèi xiàlái le) — Work assignments have been distributed. |
| Action continues from past to present (legacy, inheritance) | 这些古书都是爷爷遗留下来的。(Zhèxiē gǔshū dōu shì yéye yíliú xiàlái de) — These old books were left behind by my grandfather. |
| Separate or detach something | 他把那机器上的零部件拆卸下来了。(Tā bǎ nà jīqì shàng de líng bùjiàn chāixiè xiàlái le) — He dismantled the parts from the machine. |
| Become fixed or stabilized (from moving to still) | 红灯一亮，车都停了下来。(Hóng dēng yī liàng, chē dōu tíngle xiàlái) — When the red light came on, all cars stopped. |
| Intensity decreases — from strong to weak | 风越来越小了，风筝缓缓地飘落下来。(Fēng yuè lái yuè xiǎo le, fēngzhēng huǎnhuan de piāoluò xiàlái) — The wind weakened; the kite drifted slowly down. |

---

### 下去 xiàqù — Down away from speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move downward away from speaker (speaker is higher) | 你们别上来，我下去吧。(Nǐmen bié shànglái, wǒ xiàqù ba) — Don't come up; I'll come down. |
| Distribute downward — speaker is at higher level | 我把你们的作业发下去了。(Wǒ bǎ nǐmen de zuòyè fā xiàqù le) — I've distributed your homework. |
| Action continues into the future (persistence) | 我很想在河内一直住下去。(Wǒ hěn xiǎng zài Hénèi yīzhí zhù xiàqù) — I want to keep living in Hanoi. / 你不坚持下去就不会成功。(Nǐ bù jiānchí xiàqù jiù bù huì chénggōng) — If you don't keep going, you won't succeed. |

---

### 出来 chūlái — Out toward speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move outward toward speaker (speaker is outside) | 我不进去了，你快去出来吧！(Wǒ bù jìnqù le, nǐ kuài qù chūlái ba) — I'm not going in; come out quickly! |
| **From non-existence to existence; from vague to clear** — used with 写, 画, 想, 做, 创造, 提, 找, 查, 研究 etc. | 我想出办法来了。(Wǒ xiǎng chū bànfǎ lái le) — I've thought of a solution. / 幸福的生活是用勤劳和智慧创造出来的。(Xìngfú de shēnghuó shì yòng qínláo hé zhìhuì chuàngzào chūlái de) — A happy life is created through diligence and wisdom. |
| Identify or recognise someone/something through analysis | 几年不见，我都认不出你来了。(Jǐ nián bùjiàn, wǒ dū rèn bu chū nǐ lái le) — Haven't seen you in years — I couldn't recognize you. / 我听出你的声音来了。(Wǒ tīng chū nǐ de shēngyīn lái le) — I recognized your voice. |

---

### 出去 chūqù — Out away from speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move outward away from speaker (speaker is inside) | 你快出去！(Nǐ kuài chūqù) — Get out quickly! |
| Something secret becomes public; something private becomes known | 这个坏消息决不能透露出去。(Zhège huài xiāoxī jué bùnéng tòulù chūqù) — This bad news must not leak out. / 这是我们的秘密，千万别说出去。(Zhè shì wǒmen de mìmì, qiān wàn bié shuō chūqù) — This is our secret — never tell anyone. |

---

### 过来 guòlái — Across toward speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move from far to near (toward speaker) | 你快跑过来吧！(Nǐ kuài pǎo guòlái ba) — Run over here quickly! |
| **From abnormal/bad state → normal/good state** | 老师讲了半天我才明白过来。(Lǎoshī jiǎng le bàntiān wǒ cái míngbái guòlái) — After the teacher explained for a long time, I finally understood. / 很幸运！他活过来了！(Hěn xìngyùn! Tā huó guòlái le) — How lucky — he came back to life! |
| Transfer or translate from one place/form to another | 这本中文小说是翻译过来的。(Zhè běn Zhōngwén xiǎoshuō shì fānyì guòlái de) — This Chinese novel was translated. |
| Survive or get through a difficult period | 那么艰难的日子，我们都熬过来了。(Nàme jiānnán de rìzi, wǒmen dōu áo guòlái le) — We survived those difficult days. |
| Able to handle a workload (with 得/不) | 工作太多，我一个人忙不过来。(Gōngzuò tài duō, wǒ yīgè rén máng bu guòlái) — Too much work — I can't manage alone. / 我们三个人一起做，一定忙得过来。(Wǒmen sān gèrén yīqǐ zuò, yīdìng máng de guòlái) — With three of us, we can definitely handle it. |

---

### 过去 guòqù — Across away from speaker

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: move from near to far (away from speaker) | 你走过去吧。(Nǐ zǒu guòqù ba) — Walk over there. |
| **From good/normal state → bad/abnormal state** | 他太累了！昏过去了。(Tā tài lèi le! Hūn guòqù le) — He was so exhausted — he passed out! |
| Event or period has ended / passed | 中秋节已经过去了。(Zhōngqiū jié yǐjīng guòqù le) — Mid-Autumn Festival has already passed. |
| Evaluation: barely acceptable (with 得/不) | 这条裙子看得过去。(Zhè tiáo qúnzi kàn de guòqù) — That skirt looks okay, I suppose. / 这样的成绩也太说不过去了。(Zhèyàng de chéngjī yě tài shuō bu guòqù le) — Results like these just don't cut it. |

---

### 起来 qǐlái — Upward from a lower position

| Meaning | Examples |
|---|---|
| Literal: rise from lower to higher position | 小王站起来。(Xiǎowáng zhàn qǐlái) — Xiao Wang stood up. / 他从椅子上站起来。(Tā cóng yǐzi shàng zhàn qǐlái) — He got up from the chair. |
| **Action begins and gains momentum** | 命令一下达，大家马上行动起来。(Mìnglìng yīxià dá, dàjiā mǎshàng xíngdòng qǐlái) — As soon as the order came, everyone sprang into action. / 音乐响起，大家跳起舞来。(Yīnyuè xiǎngqǐ, dàjiā tiào qǐ wǔ lái) — The music started; everyone began to dance. |
| Intensity increases — from weak to strong | 天亮了起来。(Tiānliàng le qǐlái) — It's gradually getting light. / 他一到，会场立刻热闹起来了。(Tā yī dào, huìchǎng lìkè rènào qǐlái le) — The moment he arrived, the venue got lively. |
| Gather, collect, or accumulate | 经验都是一点点积累起来的。(Jīngyàn dōu shì yī diǎndiǎn jīlěi qǐlái de) — Experience is built up little by little. / 把桌子上的书都收起来吧。(Bǎ zhuōzi shàng de shū dōu shōu qǐlái ba) — Gather up all the books on the table. |
| **Recall something forgotten** | 一时糊涂做了错事，现在想起来悔恨不已。(Yīshí hútú zuòle cuò shì, xiànzài xiǎng qǐlái huǐhèn bu yǐ) — Made a mistake in confusion; now remembering it I'm full of regret. |
| **Evaluation or assessment** — "when you V, it feels/seems..." | 这双鞋子穿起来很舒服。(Zhè shuāng xiézi chuān qǐlái hěn shūfu) — These shoes are very comfortable to wear. / 看起来很容易，做起来很难。(Kàn qǐlái hěn róngyì, zuò qǐlái hěn nán) — Looks easy; doing it is hard. |
| Time reference — "when speaking of / when doing X" | 说起这件事来，我就生气。(Shuō qǐ zhè jiàn shì lái, wǒ jiù shēngqì) — When this topic comes up, I get angry. |

## Object Placement Rules

Object placement is **strict**. The rules differ by complement type.

### Rule 1: Simple 来/去 — six sub-cases

| Object type | Position | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Regular noun | Before **or** after 来/去 (both valid) | 他带来了两斤水果 ≈ 他带了两斤水果来 |
| Location noun | **Before** 来/去 | 小王回学校去了 · 老师走进教室来 |
| Abstract noun | **After** 来/去 | 语言不通，会带来很多困难 |
| Future/unfulfilled action | **Before** 来/去 | 晚上朋友聚会，他想带一些巧克力去 |
| Imperative / command | **Before** 来/去 | 你们带一本词典来！· 快倒水来 |
| Separable verb object | Between verb parts + before 来/去 | 他俩聊起天儿来 · 她低下头去 |

### Rule 2: 下去 as complement — no object after the complement

Object must move **before the verb**. Use 把 if needed.

- ✅ 经理把这些工作都安排下去了。(The manager arranged all this work.)
- ✅ 英语我还要学下去。(I still need to keep studying English.)
- ❌ ~~学下去英语~~

### Rule 3: 起来 as complement — object goes **between** 起 and 来

- 德福抽起烟来。(Défú chōu qǐ yān lái) — Defu started smoking.
- 突然下起雨来了。(Túrán xià qǐ yǔ lái le) — It suddenly started raining.

---

## Aspect Marker 了 Placement

| Sentence type | 了 position | Example |
|---|---|---|
| No object | After verb, before complement **OR** at sentence end (both valid) | 看见老师，我们都站起来了 ≈ 我们都站了起来 |
| Location object | Sentence-final | 同学们都走出教室来了 |
| Thing object | **Immediately after** the complement, before the object | 他买回来了一本古代汉语的书 |

## Negation and Questions

**Negation — past:** use 没
- 我们都没想出来什么好办法。(We couldn't think of a good solution.)

**Negation — hypothetical / general:** use 不
- 你不坚持下去就不会成功。(If you don't persevere, you won't succeed.)

**Potential form** (can/cannot do): Verb + **得/不** + directional complement
- 我想了半天还是想不起来。(I thought hard but still couldn't remember.)
- 工作太多，我一个人忙不过来。(Too much work — I can't handle it alone.)
- 每个问题都答得上来。(Can answer every question.)

**Question — simple:** add 了吗？ or 了没有 at the end
- 这个词老师昨天讲过了，你想起来了没有？(The teacher covered this word yesterday — do you remember it?)

**Question — affirmative-negative (正反问):** V + 没 + V (repeated) + Object + Complement
- 你带没带字典来？(Did you bring a dictionary or not?)

## Quick Contrast Table

| Complement | Toward speaker | Away from speaker |
|---|---|---|
| 上 | 上来 (up ↑ → me) | 上去 (up ↑ → away) |
| 下 | 下来 (down ↓ → me) | 下去 (down ↓ → away) |
| 出 | 出来 (out → me) | 出去 (out → away) |
| 过 | 过来 (across → me) | 过去 (across → away) |
| 起 | 起来 (up from below — always toward "up") | — |

**Extended meaning shorthand:**
- 上来/上去 — level rising (who is higher determines 来/去)
- 下来/下去 — level falling · 下来 = past→now; 下去 = now→future
- 出来 — things coming into existence or becoming clear
- 出去 — secrets or things going public
- 过来 — bad→good; surviving hardship; capacity to handle
- 过去 — good→bad; events ending; barely passing
- 起来 — starting an action; recalling; evaluating; gathering

**Teaching priority:** Teach 来/去 first → then 起来 (most frequency, most meanings) → then 出来/下来 → then the rest. Don't front-load all nine at once.
