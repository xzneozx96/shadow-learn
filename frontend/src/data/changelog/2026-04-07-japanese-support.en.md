**日本語対応しました!** ShadowLearn now officially supports Japanese. We’ve brought the immersive, video-first learning experience you know from Chinese to Japanese, rebuilt from the ground up to perfectly fit its unique structure.

![Japanese support screenshot](docs/images/japanese_lesson.png)

## Key Features:
- **Intelligent Furigana**: Kanji are automatically annotated with readings, so you always know how to pronounce what you read.
- **Full script support**: Seamless integration of Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji across all transcripts, exercises, and your vocabulary workbook.
- **Fully Automated**: Just paste a YouTube link or upload a file — our system instantly detects and processes everything for you.
- **Comprehensive Practice**: All exercise types (fill-in-the-blank, writing, pronunciation scoring, and shadowing) are ready for Japanese.

## Get started now:
1. Head over to **Library → New Lesson**.
2. Add your Japanese YouTube link or upload your audio/video file.
3. ShadowLearn automatically transcribes, generates furigana, and prepares all your study materials.
