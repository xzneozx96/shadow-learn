Pronunciation exercises now work inside **Workbook SRS** review sessions.
Speak your answer, get scored, and track your accuracy over time.

## How it works

1. Open the Workbook and start an SRS review session.
2. When a pronunciation exercise appears, tap the microphone.
3. Speak the word or phrase — you'll get an instant score.
4. Your accuracy is tracked and feeds back into the SRS scheduling.
