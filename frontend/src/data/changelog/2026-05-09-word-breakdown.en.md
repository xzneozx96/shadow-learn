Ever stared at a Chinese character and drawn a complete blank? **Word Breakdown** gives you "X-ray vision" into every character—stripping away the mystery by revealing its building blocks and weaving a story that makes it stick.

![Word Breakdown feature screenshot — showing radical breakdown panel and AI story for a character](docs/images/word_breakdown_demo.png)
*← Click the book icon on any vocabulary card to open this panel*

## 🔬 Master Character Anatomy

Every Chinese character is a puzzle built from smaller pieces—radicals and components that carry meaning and sound. Word Breakdown lays it all bare:

- **Radicals** — The semantic roots that tip you off to a word's category or meaning.
- **Components** — Every sub-part of the character, named and explained.
- **Hán Việt (Sino-Vietnamese)** — The bridge that connects characters to the Vietnamese vocabulary you already know.

Stop treating characters like arbitrary squiggles. Once you see the pieces, the whole finally makes sense.

## 🧠 AI Stories: Glue It to Your Brain

Definitions are forgettable. Vivid stories are not. The moment you open the breakdown panel, our AI **auto-weaves** the character's components into a memorable scene—something bizarre, funny, or dramatic enough that you'll never blank on this word again.

Not feeling the vibe? Hit 🔄 for a fresh story, or ✏️ to pen your own. Your story is saved directly to the card, ready for every future review session.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open your **Vocabulary Workbook** from any lesson.
2. Click the **book icon** (📖) on any Chinese word card.
3. The breakdown opens and the AI **instantly generates** a unique memory story.
4. Scroll down to explore the **component tree**: see exactly how the character is built.
5. Not happy with the story? Hit 🔄 to regenerate, or ✏️ to write your own.
6. Your story is saved and ready whenever you're back to review.
