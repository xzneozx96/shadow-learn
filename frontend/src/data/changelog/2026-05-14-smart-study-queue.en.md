Consistency is the hardest part of language learning. The new **Smart Study** handles the logistics so you can focus on what actually matters: practicing.

## 📋 Your Daily Goals, Always in Sight
A floating badge on every page keeps you focused on your progress. Tap it to reveal your study dashboard—a streamlined view of your daily reviews, shadowing sessions, and custom tasks. Stay organized without ever leaving your flow.

## 🧠 Spaced Repetition, Automated
Every word you study is automatically tracked and scheduled using **Spaced Repetition (SRS)**. The queue intelligently surfaces words right before they fade from memory, ensuring long-term retention without any manual setup.

## 🎯 Five Skills, One Seamless Flow
The Daily Review session covers everything: Vocabulary, Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. Use the interactive progress ring to track your journey, and feel free to jump between skills or pick up exactly where you left off later.

## ✅ Tailored to Your Learning
Not every task fits a template. Add your own goals—like conversation practice or grammar notes—and check them off as you go. Your daily queue, built around the way you learn.
