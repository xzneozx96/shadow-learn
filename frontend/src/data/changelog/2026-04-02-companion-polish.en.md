We've upgraded your learning companion to make your study sessions more seamless and effective.

## Key Improvements:
- **Zero-lag chat**: The chat input is now fully responsive, even when the AI is streaming long responses. No more waiting to type your next question.
- **Enhanced memory**: Your assistant now has an "extended memory." Context is no longer lost after a few exchanges, allowing for deeper, more cohesive conversations.
- **Stop button & Auto-scroll**: The latest messages stay in view in real-time, and you can now hit **Stop** to cut off any response the moment you've got the info you need.

Focus better and eliminate interruptions — available now in both the lesson companion panel and the full-screen chat.
