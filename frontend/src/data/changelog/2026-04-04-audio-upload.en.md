No more limits. You can now create lessons from any audio file, not just videos.

Simply drag and drop your file into ShadowLearn, and the system handles the entire pipeline: transcription, segmentation, translation, and vocabulary extraction — exactly as it would for a video.

## Get started with:
- **Podcasts**: Use your favorite episodes to study conversations segment by segment.
- **Audiobooks**: Transform any chapter into an intensive shadowing session.
- **Tutor recordings**: Upload voice notes for a full transcript and vocabulary breakdown.
- **Radio/Short clips**: Challenge yourself with real, unscripted speech.
