## 🔗 One‑Click Video Import

Paste a YouTube URL — a single video or an entire playlist. ShadowLearn automatically detects the type, fetches the title, thumbnail, and metadata, then adds it neatly to your library. Tag it by skill (Grammar, Vocabulary, Pronunciation, Speaking, or Learning Tips) and instruction language so you can find it exactly where you left it.

No complex setup. No waiting. Study on your own terms.

## 🧠 AI Study Companion

Every video in your collection — yours or ours — opens the same powerful toolkit.

- **AI Summary** – Key grammar points and vocabulary distilled before you press play.
- **Mind Map** – Visualize how grammar structures and vocabulary connect across the lesson.

- **Study Guide** – Challenge yourself with grammar rules, usage patterns, and concept explanations built from the video.
- **Flashcard Deck** – Drill vocabulary and grammar patterns straight from the transcript.

- **Chat** – Ask grammar questions, clarify vocabulary in context — every answer anchored in what was actually said.
- **Notes** – Save grammar rules, example sentences, and key vocabulary. Searchable anytime.
