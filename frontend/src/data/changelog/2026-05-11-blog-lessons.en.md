YouTube isn't the only way to learn. Now, you can turn **any article you find interesting** into a high-quality audio podcast — complete with AI-generated narration, aligned transcripts, and vocabulary breakdowns for deep shadowing practice.

## 📰 Your Own Personal Podcast
Just paste a link from any news site or blog. ShadowLearn will automatically extract the content and generate a natural-sounding audio version. It's like having a custom podcast created just for you, featuring the topics you actually care about — from tech blogs to global news.

## ✍️ No Link? No Problem.
Found a great snippet on your phone or an article behind a login wall? Switch to **Paste Text** mode and drop in up to 2,000 characters. We'll handle the rest — synthesizing the voice, creating the transcript, and translating the text — so you can start shadowing immediately.

## 🎬 Study with a Vibe
Your personal podcasts now come with a visual mood. Enjoy looping, ambient background videos that create the perfect study atmosphere. Pick your favorite scene from the bottom strip, and click anywhere on the video to instantly control the audio.
